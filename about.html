About Us

Dedicated to Academic Excellence

Who We Are

telMAX Thonnakkal is the premier tuition institute in Trivandrum, specifically designed for students from Class 5 to 10. As a school section of telnet, we bring a legacy of trust and quality education.

We specialize in the State syllabus, ensuring that every student receives the focused attention they need to excel. Our English medium-only classes foster an environment of professional growth and improved communication skills.

Our Vision

To empower students with knowledge and confidence to face academic challenges and build a strong foundation for their future.

Teaching Method

Interactive learning, regular assessments, and personalized attention to ensure every concept is thoroughly understood.
